# Verification Checklist

## Purpose

Standardize reusable verification review across bounded seams.

## When to use

Use before declaring validation complete or before requesting review.

## Required inputs

- active motion or seam
- acceptance criteria
- required gates
- changed or proposed outputs

## Expected outputs

- explicit pass/fail or open-item notes

## Checklist

- confirm acceptance criteria are each addressed
- confirm required gates are listed and run when applicable
- confirm observed results are recorded accurately
- confirm boundaries were preserved
- confirm no fabricated evidence or status claims

## Hard boundaries

- do not mark unrun checks as passing
- do not collapse warnings into pass without stating them

# Operator Review Checklist

## Purpose

Standardize operator review of authority, boundaries, and escalation posture.

## When to use

Use when a seam touches authority-sensitive planning, docs-ops levels, routing,
or escalation language.

## Required inputs

- active motion
- requested authority level
- settled canon
- proposed outputs

## Expected outputs

- explicit approval, rejection, or deferral notes

## Checklist

- confirm the requested level is actually in scope
- confirm no higher authority is implied by template or draft text
- confirm no branch, PR, merge, or execution authority is added accidentally
- confirm blocked outputs remain blocked
- confirm future-modeled levels remain disabled when required

## Hard boundaries

- do not treat reusable assets as authority
- do not approve by omission

# Risk Register Checklist

## Purpose

Provide a reusable way to surface concrete risks and mitigations for bounded seams.

## When to use

Use when documenting challenge posture, review notes, or closeout risk summary.

## Required inputs

- scope
- known constraints
- proposed outputs

## Expected outputs

- explicit risk and mitigation notes

## Checklist

- identify at least the main ambiguity or authority risks
- state why the risk matters
- state the mitigation or containment boundary
- state whether the risk remains open or resolved

## Hard boundaries

- do not hide unresolved authority risks
- do not restate generic risk text without seam-specific meaning

## Related canon

- motion challenge patterns

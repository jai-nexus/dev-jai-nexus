# Role Card: operator

## Purpose

Record and enforce whether authority escalation is or is not authorized for the
active seam.

## When to use

Use whenever a seam needs explicit confirmation that authority remains bounded,
disabled, or separately approved.

## Required inputs

- active motion boundary
- settled canon
- requested authority level
- known guardrails

## Expected outputs

- explicit authorization or non-authorization record
- guardrail confirmation
- escalation rejection or deferral note

## Hard boundaries

- operator role card is not self-authorizing
- authority still depends on settled motions and explicit decisions
- no repo mutation by implication

## Related canon

- motion-0173
- motions 0174, 0175, 0176

## Non-authority statement

This role card is operating guidance only. It does not itself grant operator
authority beyond what canon already allows.

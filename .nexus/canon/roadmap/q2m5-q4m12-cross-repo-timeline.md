# Q2M5-Q4M12 Cross-Repo Canonical Timeline

## Purpose

This timeline maps the Q2M5 through Q4M12 roadmap across repos/domains without
declaring `dev-jai-nexus` the sole owner of all canon.

`dev-jai-nexus` is the Operator Control Plane. It coordinates visibility,
routing, deliberation, passalongs, and cross-repo posture. It does not absorb
all Golden Script, NHID, audit, hygiene, ritual-assessment, or product canon.

Ownership assumptions below are provisional until each target repo/domain is
inspected and later ratified in its own lane.

# Decision Record - motion-0035

## Decision
Proceed with the first formal context substrate for dev-jai-nexus.

## Reason
Motion-0033 proved that dev-jai-nexus can generate reusable context bundles, but those bundles still needed a governed substrate defining what project identity, repo capsules, motion packets, slot policy, and scoring standards are supposed to mean. Motion-0035 closes that gap by adding the first constitutional and schema-level substrate artifacts.

## Constraints
- keep the substrate compact and governance-oriented,
- preserve existing JAI NEXUS terminology,
- keep slot policy role-first rather than provider-first,
- avoid expansion into retrieval, embeddings, or cross-repo federation,
- treat these artifacts as substrate contracts rather than replacements for canonical motion records.

## Ratification condition
Ratify only after all five substrate artifacts exist and can be read together as a coherent constitutional context pack:
- project constitution,
- repo capsule schema,
- motion packet schema,
- slot policy,
- scoring rubric.

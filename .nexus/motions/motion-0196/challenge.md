# Challenge: Corpus V1 Closeout and Corpus Model v0

## Risks

- a corpus-reset policy could be misread as immediate authorization to renumber
- Corpus V2 language could be overread as proof that live agent governance
  exists already
- closeout framing could accidentally weaken Corpus V1 historical preservation

## Mitigations

- state explicitly that Corpus V2 is not open yet
- state explicitly that numbering reset policy is future-facing only
- preserve Corpus V1 as historical canon and allow only explicit hygiene fixes
- keep the seam canon-first and read-only

# Challenge: Paid Beta Operator Summary Surface v0

## Challenge

The root page could become cluttered or imply an operational paid-beta panel if
the surface is too large.

## Response

The summary stays compact and explicit:

- paid beta not open
- payment not authorized
- customer data not authorized
- production not selected
- local machines not customer-serving
- read-only routing summary

No controls, state mutation, or runtime checks are introduced.

# Challenge: Corpus V2 Pre-Opening Operator Checklist v0

## Risks

- a pre-opening checklist could be mistaken for opening approval
- checklist completion could be overread as enough to skip later motions
- operator-facing evidence requirements could become stale

## Mitigations

- state explicitly that the checklist is pre-opening only
- require later explicit motionization for opening, reset, and authority changes
- require current evidence review before any future opening proposal

# Proposal: Paid Beta Unit Economics Boundary v0

## Purpose

Define planning-only unit-economics guardrails for a future paid beta before
pricing becomes externally committed.

## Scope

- add a paid-beta unit-economics boundary artifact under `.nexus/canon/`
- record bounded-economics principles and cost-exposure categories
- define plan-envelope requirements for future paid-beta planning
- capture open questions that remain unresolved
- preserve planning-only posture and non-goals explicitly
- refresh the bundled motion snapshot through `motion-0230`

## Non-Goals

- no final external pricing
- no paid beta opening
- no payment collection
- no live Stripe
- no auth implementation
- no backend persistence
- no provider/model calls
- no runtime execution
- no mutation of `jai-nexus`, `api-nexus`, or any other repo

# Challenge: Paid Beta Unit Economics Boundary v0

## Challenge

The main risk is allowing vague plan language or “unlimited” framing to create
unbounded variable-cost exposure before commercial ownership and infrastructure
readiness exist.

## Response

This boundary stays planning-only and explicit:

- unlimited AI economics are rejected
- cost-exposure categories are listed directly
- expensive usage must be bounded or metered
- enterprise remains contracted/custom
- free or sandbox plans cannot imply unlimited variable-cost exposure
- no external commercial commitment is created

That gives future pricing work a disciplined boundary without prematurely
committing product or infrastructure behavior.

# Challenge: Paid Beta Readiness Gate v0

## Challenge

The main risk is treating product-readiness, placeholder billing, or company
formation milestones as if they already satisfy the minimum requirements for a
real paid beta.

## Response

This gate definition keeps the distinction explicit:

- paid beta is not open
- required owners and infrastructure choices remain future work
- privacy/security and customer-data boundaries remain unsatisfied
- review-required product posture must be preserved
- no live Stripe, auth, backend, persistence, or provider behavior is authorized

That creates a disciplined pre-launch checklist without falsely claiming the
company is ready to serve real customers.

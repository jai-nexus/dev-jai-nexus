# Challenge: Corpus V2 Readiness Taxonomy v0

## Challenge

Defining Corpus V2 too loosely could be misread as permission to open it soon
or to treat agent operability as implicit execution authority.

## Response

The taxonomy keeps the boundary explicit:

- Corpus V2 is not opened
- numbering is not reset
- readiness gates are future requirements only
- agent operability is distinct from execution authority
- dry-run and evidence substrates remain governed inputs only

# Challenge: Corpus V2 Readiness Gate v0

## Risks

- readiness language could be mistaken for enablement
- voting-role language could blur governance identities with execution-lens
  identities
- workflow-ready output language could be overread as authority expansion

## Mitigations

- state explicitly that Corpus V2 remains gated and unopened
- require vote records to distinguish governance voters, execution/lens agents,
  and human intervention
- preserve human override boundary
- preserve the no branch-write, PR, merge, or execution authority posture

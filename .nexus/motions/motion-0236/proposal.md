# Proposal: Paid Beta Next-Routing Decision v0

## Purpose

Record the next recommended paid-beta route after the readiness matrix and
status-model work.

## Scope

- add a next-routing decision artifact
- compare the likely cross-repo next-route options
- recommend one next route
- keep the decision routing-only and non-authorizing

## Non-goals

- mutating any destination repo
- authorizing paid beta
- authorizing customer-facing offer publication
- authorizing billing, auth, persistence, customer data, or provider/model behavior

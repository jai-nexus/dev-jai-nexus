## Summary

Add a manual draft-review validation checklist that operators can use to confirm the simulated draft fixture remains clear, bounded, and explicitly non-authoritative.

## Why

The prototype needs a concise review checklist so operators can evaluate fixture quality without treating the fixture as canon or runtime state.

## Scope

- add a checklist canon artifact
- cover identity, scope, non-goals, contributors, validation expectations, and authority boundary
- optionally summarize the checklist on `/operator/corpus`

## Non-Goals

- automation
- runtime enforcement
- live draft generation
- fixture promotion to canon

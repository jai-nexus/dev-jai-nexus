# Challenge: Static Motion Draft Fixture v0

## Risks

- a fixture draft could be misread as a real proposal
- placeholder IDs could look like active Corpus V2 numbering
- role/lens contributors could be confused with live voter identities

## Mitigations

- label the fixture as simulated, review-only, and not canon
- use placeholder corpus and motion IDs explicitly
- preserve the distinction between proposer identity and role/lens contributors

# Challenge: Agent Motion Drafting Design v0

## Risks

- a future drafting design could be overread as active implementation
- draft roles could blur governance voters with lens contributors
- draft states could look authoritative without explicit human review

## Mitigations

- state future-state posture explicitly
- separate drafting roles from governance voter identities
- preserve human operator boundaries
- require deterministic and schema-valid outputs before any future opening

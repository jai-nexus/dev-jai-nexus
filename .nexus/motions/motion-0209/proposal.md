# Proposal: Agent Governance Sandbox Boundary v0

## Purpose

Define the future no-provider and no-authority sandbox boundary for
agent-operable motion drafting and voting experiments.

## Scope

- add canon defining the sandbox boundary
- define allowed future sandbox materials
- define disallowed authority and fixture posture
- relate the boundary to readiness and the drafting/voting design seams

## Non-goals

- no live drafting
- no live voting
- no autonomous ratification
- no provider/model dispatch
- no runtime execution
- no Corpus V2 opening or numbering reset

## Reserved portfolio sequence

The sequence is a dependency order, not a roadmap commitment. Every stage is
`RESERVED / NOT_ROUTED / NOT_OPEN`.

| Order | Reserved stage | Minimum future decision boundary | Current posture |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | Current-state and authority reconciliation | Reconcile current canon against legacy Program, Agent, model-slot, and mirror claims | NOT_ROUTED |
| 2 | Required five-slot governance architecture | Define exact slot identities, role separation, provider neutrality, evidence, cost, safety, and human authority | NOT_ROUTED |
| 3 | JAI core activation readiness | Define identity, capabilities, memory, tools, supervision, audit, rollback, and explicit activation gates | NOT_ROUTED |
| 4 | JAI Agent activation readiness | Define per-Agent identity/NHID policy, scope, tools, memory, isolation, review, and staged activation gates | NOT_ROUTED |
| 5 | JAI Council activation readiness | Define membership, quorum, dissent, conflicts, evidence, human override, audit, and non-automatic authority | NOT_ROUTED |

No stage inherits authorization from an earlier stage. Completion of one stage
does not route, open, accept, execute, or activate the next.

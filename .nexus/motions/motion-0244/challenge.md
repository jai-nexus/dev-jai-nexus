# Challenge: Customer-Configurable Corpus Model v0

## Challenge

Extending corpus semantics toward customer/project compartments could be
misread as existing product functionality or as a change to the internal Corpus
V1/V2 governance model.

## Response

The model keeps the boundary explicit:

- internal Corpus V1/V2 posture is preserved
- customer/project corpus behavior is future-only
- review and archive posture are conceptual only
- no customer persistence or UI exists
- no runtime, auth, billing, or data behavior is introduced

# Challenge: Corpus V2 Readiness Checklist v0

## Risks

- a checklist could be misread as evidence that Corpus V2 is nearly open when
  several gates remain explicitly unmet
- canon-only gate satisfaction could be conflated with runtime operability
- authority-blocked capabilities could look like implementation gaps instead of
  intentional governance boundaries

## Mitigations

- define a narrow status model that distinguishes canon satisfaction from live
  operability
- list current evidence, missing requirements, and authority notes for every
  gate
- state explicitly that Corpus V2 remains gated, unopened, and unreset
- keep the seam docs-only and canon-only with no runtime or authority change

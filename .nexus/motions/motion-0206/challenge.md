# Challenge: Corpus V2 Opening Protocol Draft v0

## Risks

- an opening protocol draft could be mistaken for actual opening approval
- numbering-reset planning could be conflated with a live reset
- readiness waivers could be under-specified if the protocol is too loose

## Mitigations

- state explicitly that Corpus V2 is not opened here
- require explicit readiness evidence and waiver treatment in a future opening
  motion
- require future control-thread proof before any reset or opening action

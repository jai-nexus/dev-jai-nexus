# Proposal: Corpus V2 Opening Protocol Draft v0

## Purpose

Draft the future protocol for opening Corpus V2 without actually opening it.

## Scope

- add canon defining opening preconditions
- define required proof and evidence for a future opening motion
- define required output artifacts for the eventual opening step
- define future `CONTROL_THREAD` decision requirements
- keep the seam protocol-only and non-opening

## Non-goals

- no Corpus V2 opening
- no numbering reset
- no `motion-0001` creation
- no live drafting, voting, or ratification enablement
- no authority expansion
